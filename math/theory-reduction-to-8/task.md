# theory-reduction-to-8

## Problem

12 theories in `core/theories/` create cognitive overhead
and overlaps. Two cycles of past dialogues established that
8 is the sweet spot: fewer than 8 loses expressiveness,
more than 8 creates redundancy.

## Desired outcome

`core/theories/` lists exactly 8 `.md` files: curry-howard,
predicate, fsm, refinement, verdict, epistemic, deprecation,
agent.

## Constraints

- No loss of conceptual coverage — drop material is
  absorbed into foundational theories.
- Tag v0.618 stays untouched (its 12 theories are an
  archived genome).
