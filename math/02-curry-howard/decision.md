# 02-curry-howard

## Thesis

A packet is a proof term. A verifier is a type-check.
The five files of a packet are the structure of the proof.

## Antithesis

A packet as mere documentation cannot be enforced. A
verifier as mere linter cannot reason about the proposition.

## Synthesis

A2 is the bridge that A0 (Difference) makes necessary. The
five-file packet (manifest, proposition, intent, knowledge,
refinement) is the Curry-Howard image of a typed lambda-term.
